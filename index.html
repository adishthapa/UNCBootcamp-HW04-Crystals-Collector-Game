Crystals Collector!

Directions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals to the side. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You will win the game by matching your total score to the random number and you will loose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystals is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time when the game starts, the game will change the value of each of the crystals.
